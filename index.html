Pricing

Prices depend on the language direction, document type, length, formatting, urgency and whether a certified translation statement is required.

Standard Translation

From £30 per page for straightforward Spanish ↔ English document translation where no certified statement, sworn translator signature or official stamp is required.

Certified Translation Statement

From £40 per page where a UK certified translation statement is required and accepted by the receiving authority.

Longer Documents

From £0.10 per word for longer documents, depending on complexity, formatting requirements and deadline.

All prices are starting prices. A final quote is confirmed before any work begins, based on the document type, length, formatting, deadline and certification requirements.

Minimum charge: £25. Urgent work may include an additional fee.

Draft translation support for translators, agencies and authorised professionals is available from £0.06 per word, subject to document type, review requirements and deadline.

Bolgsvenia Global is a UK-based translation service providing Spanish ↔ English translation support for individuals, businesses and professional clients.
